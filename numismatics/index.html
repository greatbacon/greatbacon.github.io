United States Federal Reserve Note two-dollar bill
USD $ 2 -- Issued in 1976
The current design of this bill began being issued & printed in 1976. Two-dollar bills do not circulate as well as other denominations of U.S. currency. This comparative scarcity in circulation, coupled with a lack of public knowledge that the bill is still in production and circulation, has also inspired urban legends about its authenticity and value and has occasionally created problems for those trying to use the bill to make purchases.
