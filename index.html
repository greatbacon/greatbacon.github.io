The Shadows Institue

"Your hub for locally sourced horror"

The Shadows Institute was founded as an experimental space for implementing peer production models for horror fiction.

We seek to take back the power of the pen & purse from the ruthless suits that ruin creative pursuits and put it back in the hands of the creators.

Our primary publication is currently a quarterly zine, "Shadows of the Rocky Mountains", which focuses on small horror stories set in and around the area.
